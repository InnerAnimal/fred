Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a mobility grant? +

Start by completing our online application form. You'll need to provide medical documentation, financial information, and a detailed explanation of your needs. Our team will review your application and contact you within 2-3 business days.

What types of equipment are covered? +

We cover wheelchairs, adaptive vehicles, home modifications, assistive technology, and other mobility aids. Each application is evaluated based on individual needs and available funding.

How long does the application process take? +

Most applications are processed within 2-4 weeks. Emergency situations may be expedited. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

Is there a limit to how much assistance I can receive? +

Grant amounts vary based on need and available funding. Most grants range from $500 to $5,000. We work with you to find the best solution within our budget.

Can I apply for multiple programs? +

Yes! Many of our participants benefit from multiple programs. We encourage you to explore all available resources that can help improve your quality of life.
